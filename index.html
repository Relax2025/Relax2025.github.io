RELAX 2025

Workshop on Relaxed Semantics in Data Analytics Pipelines

Co-located with DEBS 2025 — June 10, 2025

Scope

The RELAX 2025 workshop focuses on developing scalable and efficient data-intensive software systems that can navigate trade-offs between strict correctness guarantees and system performance. We aim to foster discussions on designing flexible data management systems that balance decentralization, approximation, and ethics while addressing challenges of high-velocity and diverse data.

Topics of Interest

  • Trade-offs between precision and computational efficiency
  • Federated learning and distributed AI for data analytics
  • Robust and resilient AI-driven data pipelines
  • Scalable event-based and distributed computing systems
  • Bias detection, mitigation, and fairness in AI processing
  • Ethical considerations in approximate computing
  • Sustainable AI and energy-efficient data analytics

Submission Guidelines

We welcome submissions in two formats:

  • Regular Papers: Up to 6 pages, including references.
  • Short Papers: Up to 4 pages, suitable for preliminary or position papers.
Submissions must follow the ACM "sigconf" template (10pt) and be submitted via EasyChair. All papers will undergo single-blind peer review. Accepted papers will be published in the proceedings and authors of selected papers may be invited to a special issue of Elsevier's *Information Systems*.
